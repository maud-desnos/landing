At the beginning of an era affected by climate change and increasing global warming, we should be more aware about the consequences of exposure to the sun. Did you know that 50% to 70% of skin cancers are directly related to an overexposure to UVA and UVB radiation and that 1 in 5 Americans developed them ?

We built a solution for you. This, is called UV-action. You put it on your clothes and it calculates the UV index we are exposed to, and tells us whether we need to put sunscreen every 2, 4 or 6 hours depending on the intensity of those rays.

The electronic chip/patch would be linked to you by an application on your phone which offers you two services : first inform you about your exposure to UV with three different colours depending on their intensity and harmfulness (green/yellow/red). The second one suggests some sun-cream, produced by our company and adapted to the current weather....
